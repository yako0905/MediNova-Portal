Welcome to our Healthcare Information Portal

a centralized platform dedicated to simplifying healthcare access for everyone. Whether you're searching for hospitals, medical specialties, or expert doctors, our website provides a user-friendly experience to help you make informed health decisions.

This project was developed as part of an academic initiative to combine web development skills with real-world problem-solving. We aim to demonstrate how technology can bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers by offering:

🔍 Smart Search for Doctors & Hospitals
🏥 Detailed Information on Specialties & Facilities
📅 Easy Appointment Booking Interface
🌐 Access for International Patients
